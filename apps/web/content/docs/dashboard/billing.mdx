<Callout type="info" title="Coming soon">
The Billing area isn't available yet. Openship Cloud pricing and billing are
still being finalized, and pricing will be published before Cloud becomes paid.
</Callout>

## Self-hosted has no billing

A plain **self-hosted** Openship has nothing to pay and shows no billing data —
the Billing area simply doesn't appear. You only pay for your own infrastructure.

## Openship Cloud

Once Openship Cloud launches, the Billing area will let you watch your usage,
manage your plan, and view invoices. Until then there's nothing to configure.

Want a heads-up when it's ready? [Get in touch](/contact).
