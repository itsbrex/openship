## Self-hosted

A self-hosted Openship has **no billing** — there's nothing to pay and no
subscription, usage, or top-up endpoints to call. You only pay for your own
infrastructure.

## Openship Cloud

When Openship Cloud launches, this page will document the Stripe-backed
subscription, metered usage, one-shot credit top-ups, and the billing portal.
